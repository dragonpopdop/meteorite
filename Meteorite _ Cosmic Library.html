Chapter 1: Earrings

Discipline is considered one of the strongest things to learn to truly survive in the world, but some people are just naturally gifted to not need discipline, and that's what makes the world seem unfair. Life can be super unfair; this is why I believe the goal of every human is to make the unfair world work in their favor. - Probably someone

After getting fired from every job imaginable, I realize that I may need to start developing at least a small amount of discipline. “Man walking is so annoying, why am I even doing thi-” I say before being cut off by a piece of paper landing next to me, “HELP WANTED Detective wanted for the Seattle Police Department, looking for TOP detectives, willing to pay $140,000 a year.” Well the wages are pretty good, but a detective job is way too much effort, I couldn’t even take orders. This is also way too convenient, almost like a writer was too lazy to come up with good foreshadowing or something.

“SOMEONE STOP HIM” A woman screamed in the distance. I saw a woman get her diamond studded purse stolen, but it's strange how someone who clearly has money had zero self defense items, whatever. “Hey, do you need help?” I ask her, “YES PLEASE, THAT MAN STOLE MY PURSE” she responds loudly. Well that much was obvious, but I guess I should be kind. “Ill try my hardest to find him.” I forced myself to sound proud to say, “thank you so much sir” wow she lost that fear pretty fast, whatever, I’m bored anyway.

Sadly I wasn’t able to see what the thief looked like, nor what they were wearing, yet I’m sure a thief will stand out pretty well, but I don’t have the time to look around so lets narrow it down a little. Considering the purse is diamond studded and it is a very sunny day, I am very sure that it would have at least a small glint, since the thief is more likely to be in the shadows, I just need to look there and wait for a shine. After a few minutes I see a guy on his phone with a diamond studded purse, of course that could just be his purse, but it doesn’t fit him too well. Strangely enough, he gave me the purse without any conflict. “here is your purse ma’am” I said proudly, “thank you so much, if I may ask, what is your name?” she doesn’t seem too happy about getting her purse back, almost like it didn’t matter. “My name is Cid Law, how about you?” I asked trying to be nice, I really couldn’t care less about her name, “luluana first, but you can call me lulu” she said with a sweet look, “Well I gotta get going, Cya!” I say before jogging off. I really hope I don’t see her again.

Getting home was pretty normal aside from me forgetting to get my jacket when I got fired, of course it rained because the universe hates me, but I like the rain. I pretty much slept through the next day since I had no plans on job hunting, but the next day, I got a knock on my door “We are with the SPD, please open the door immediately” Considering I haven’t committed any crimes, and I have no criminal record, I opened the door in confusion, and that was the worst mistake I have ever made in my life.

“We are with the SPD, and you have been recommended to join the SPD ace detective union” the very obvious leader said to me, “Who would recommend me?” Normally I ask questions to seem nice, but I have no idea who could’ve possibly recommended me, and had enough power for me to get accepted with no prior experience with detective work, “That much cannot be told, if you do not come with us, then your house will be claimed” well that completely counters every single plan, so I might as well decline them once I’m there. “Okay just give me a moment”. As I close the door, I realize that I could really go for a snack, and as long as I go eventually, I can probably spend as much time as I need.

After 24 minutes of them waiting, “Hello, Cid are you there?” I see, they know my name and address, clearly this is the real deal. “Yeah, sorry, I am ready now” As we drive to the SPD detective HQ, I realize that I have never been this way a single moment in my life, but my location is the least of my concerns. I am basically being abducted because some random person decided that I would be the perfect detective. Could it have been luluana? After arriving to the HQ, I am greeted with 3 people aside from my kidnappers.

“Welcome to the HQ, my name is Charlotte Mincer” says Charlotte, “Hello, my name is Arya Cyan” said Arya, “Hello, my name is Oswald”, said four eyes, “Uh, hi, my name is cid, why am I here?” I asked. “You still don’t know, I thought this kid was supposed to be the one to outsmart everyone here?” said Oswald. “Trust me he is” said one of the officers that brought me in, “can I just know why I’m here?” I asked. “You are here because you are considered one of the smartest college students in Seattle, and our boss has decided that you will be working for our detective group” said Charlotte, “so, I am being forced to work here, and to make sure I don’t decide to leave, you are abusing government power to take away my estate?” I asked, “almost, we aren’t abusing government power, that whole taking your house thing was a lie, but if you leave then we will make sure you won’t ever have the ability to get a job” said Charlotte. “That sound a lot like abusing governmental power” I say, “Who cares, you are getting paid way more than the average American, just accept it” aggressively said by Arya, “Calm down Arya” Charlotte said, this seemed like a pretty stupid group of people, but they seem to have a good understanding of things. I seriously don’t want this job, it’s everything I hate: effort, communication, and using knowledge, but I need to take it since this is my only option. “Alright, I accept, so what do I do?” “Just follow me” said a guy in a mask, and this was the day I became a detective, the worst detective known to man.
